This research develops multi-agent systems which are robust to disturbances even while they intelligently minimize communications.

The creation of embodied world models paired with uncertainty-reducing communication/action co-planning will improve the robustness of and decrease the necessary communication for coordination of multi-agent systems in partially observable environments, increasing the utility of multi-agent systems in harsh environments.
